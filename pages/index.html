Awohali Group Consulting (AGC) is the parent company of Waya Group Consulting (WGC) dba Waya Security. Awohali is Iroquois for Eagle. AGC will coordinate and continue to add leading industry services to support each other. AGC is also owned by Brian Mastrofini a Citizen of the Cherokee Nation and his wife MaryJane Mastrofini and will giving back to the Cherokee community as it prospers to help support the nation. AGC has teamed up with Cyborus and has accepted their CEO Warren Samek as a Board Member. We will be bidding on future NASA, DOE, and DHS contracts. As the world emerges into constantly changing technologies and practices we will adapt and overcome all objectives presented in the virtual and real asymmetrical battle fields.
